Google Ads — Tracking Setup & Fix

Elite IIT · Google Search
Search Ads GTM Conversion Tracking

Google Ads was showing 14 form submissions but no email notifications were being received. The account had been running with zero conversion signal since launch — meaning the algorithm had no data to optimise against.

  • Diagnosed root cause: lead form asset collecting submissions but conversion event never set up
  • Implemented Thank You page redirect as the conversion trigger
  • Configured Google Tag Manager — trigger fires on thank you page load, sends conversion to Google Ads
  • Set test campaign at ₹200/day to verify end-to-end tracking before scaling
  • Total turnaround: 15 hours from flag to fix confirmed
