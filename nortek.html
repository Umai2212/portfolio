Social Media Strategy for Nortek Lighting

Developed comprehensive content strategy and calendars for premium lighting brand, driving consistent presence across Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest.

Client Nortek - Premium Lighting Brand
Category Social Media Marketing
Duration 3 Months
Role Digital Marketing Associate

Overview

Nortek is a premium lighting brand based in Mumbai, specializing in home decor. The project involved creating a robust social media strategy including monthly content calendars, competitive research, and design strategy across Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest.

Challenge

Nortek is a premium lighting brand with minimal social media presence competing in a crowded home decor market. They needed:

  • Consistent brand voice and visual identity across platforms
  • Strategic content plan to engage homeowners and interior designers
  • Differentiation from competitors selling similar products
  • Cost-effective content production without large agency budgets
  • Content that showcases products while providing value beyond sales pitches

Strategy & Approach

Phase 1: Competitive Research (2 weeks)

  • Analyzed 5 direct competitors in premium lighting space
  • Reviewed their full year of content across Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook
  • Identified 1,000+ posts to understand patterns, themes, seasonal campaigns
  • Documented what content types performed best: room transformations, lighting tips, installation showcases, behind-the-scenes
  • Curated design references from top-performing posts

Phase 2: Content Framework Development

Established 40/30/20/10 content distribution model:

  • 40% Educational (lighting tips, room-specific advice, design principles)
  • 30% Product showcases (features, installations, applications)
  • 20% Lifestyle & inspiration (aspirational home content, mood boards)
  • 10% Promotional (sales, new launches, special offers)

Phase 3: Content Calendar Creation

Created monthly content calendars for 3-4 months containing:

  • 15-20 content ideas per month
  • Posting schedule (3-4 posts per week on Instagram/Facebook)
  • Content format mix (static posts, carousels, reels, stories)
  • Seasonal alignment (Diwali, home renovation season, festive occasions)
  • Hashtag strategy for each post category

Phase 4: Design Direction

  • Curated Pinterest boards with 100+ design references per month
  • Organized references by content type: product focus, lifestyle, educational, promotional
  • Provided creative briefs to design team with visual direction
  • Ensured brand consistency across all content pieces

Execution

  • Collaborated with design team weekly to review upcoming content
  • Provided detailed briefs including copy suggestions, visual direction, CTA recommendations
  • Researched trending home decor topics to keep content relevant
  • Adapted calendar based on product launches and seasonal opportunities
  • Created hashtag sets targeting #interiordesign #homedecor #lightingdesign #mumbaiinteriors

Key Deliverables

  • 3-4 monthly content calendars (45-60 total content ideas)
  • Competitive analysis report documenting competitor strategies
  • 300+ curated design references organized by category
  • Content pillar framework document
  • Hashtag strategy with 30+ relevant tags per category
  • Posting schedule recommendations based on audience insights

Results

  • Client received turnkey social media strategy with 3-4 months of ready-to-execute content
  • Design team had clear creative direction reducing decision fatigue
  • Established consistent brand voice and visual identity
  • Calendar provided structure while allowing flexibility for timely content
  • Strategy informed by data-driven competitive research rather than guesswork

Project Assets

Content calendars, design references, and competitive analysis samples

Key Learnings

  • Research drives strategy - Analyzing competitors' full year revealed seasonal patterns and content gaps we could exploit
  • Quality over quantity - Better to have 15 well-researched ideas than 30 random posts
  • Visual references are essential - Designers need inspiration, not just written briefs
  • B2C premium brands need aspiration - Lifestyle and inspirational content performed better than pure product shots
  • Flexibility matters - Monthly calendars better than rigid yearly plans; allows adaptation to trends and launches

Tools Used

  • Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook for research
  • Google Sheets for calendar organization
  • Canva for design reference curation
  • Competitor profiles for benchmarking
