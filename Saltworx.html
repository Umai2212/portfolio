Meta Ads Campaign for Saltworx

Generated 6M+ impressions on ₹1,098 budget through strategic A/B testing and format optimization for B2B SaaS platform.

Client Saltworx - SaaS Platform
Category Performance Marketing
Duration 3 Weeks
Role Digital Marketing Associate

Overview

Saltworx is an inventory management SaaS platform targeting small to medium-sized businesses. The challenge was to generate brand awareness and drive website traffic with a minimal budget of ₹1,098 for the entire campaign.

The objective was to test multiple ad formats, identify top performers, and maximize reach while maintaining cost efficiency.

Challenge

The primary challenges included:

  • Limited budget (₹1,098 total) requiring strategic allocation across formats
  • Early-stage SaaS with no existing brand awareness in the market
  • Need to identify which ad format (static, carousel, video) performs best for B2B audience
  • Competing in a crowded inventory management software space
  • Balancing reach with engagement and cost-per-result optimization

Strategy & Approach

1. Budget Allocation

Divided the ₹1,098 budget strategically to test three ad formats:

  • ₹150 for Static Post (Simple, eye-catching visuals)
  • ₹150 for Carousel Ad (Multi-feature showcase)
  • ₹150 for Video Ad (Engaging product demo)
  • Remaining budget allocated to top performers after initial testing

2. Creative Development

Worked closely with design team to create platform-specific creative assets:

  • Used AI tools to generate design references and mockups
  • Researched competitor ads for messaging and visual inspiration
  • Created 3 variations per format for A/B testing
  • Focused on B2B messaging emphasizing efficiency, cost savings, and ease of use

3. Targeting Strategy

Identified and targeted decision-makers in inventory-heavy businesses:

  • Business owners and operations managers at companies with 50-500 employees
  • Interest targeting: Inventory management, supply chain, logistics, small business tools
  • Geographic focus: Major Indian metros (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune)
  • Age range: 28-55 (decision-making demographic)

Key Learnings

  • Static posts delivered maximum reach - 2.9M impressions made static format ideal for awareness campaigns despite higher CPR
  • Video ads had best engagement - Lowest CPR (₹1.74) indicated strong user interest and platform favorability
  • Carousel balanced both metrics - Middle ground between reach and engagement, useful for feature-heavy products
  • B2B requires problem-solution messaging - Ads focusing on "save time" and "reduce errors" performed better than feature lists
  • Budget constraints force smart testing - Small allocations per format allowed data-driven decisions for future scaling

Recommendations

For Future Campaigns

  • Scale video ads for engagement campaigns (best CPR)
  • Use static posts for broad awareness pushes
  • Develop carousel content for product education
  • Test geographic segmentation for cost optimization

Strategic Insights

  • Allocate 50% budget to video, 30% static, 20% carousel
  • Create urgency with limited-time offers in copy
  • Retarget engaged users with demo CTAs
  • A/B test landing pages to improve conversions
